About Apex Gym

MORE THAN
A GYM

APEX isn't just a place to work out — it's where limits are shattered. We combine cutting-edge equipment, elite coaching, and a culture of relentless progress to help every member unlock their peak performance.

Located at Sabour, Near Kotla Arab Ali Khan, Gujrat, Pakistan — founded in 2015, we've helped over 5,000 members achieve extraordinary transformations. Our state-of-the-art facility spans 25,000 sq ft with 200+ equipment pieces, dedicated zones for every discipline, recovery suites, and premium amenities.
